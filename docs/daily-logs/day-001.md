### Technical notes
- **nRF5340 DK jumperable pins**: P1.12/P1.13/P1.14 are on header P4, physically adjacent, perfect for MOSI↔MISO jumper loopback.
- **HIGH drive is mandatory**: SPIM4 @32MHz is sensitive to signal integrity; SCK/MOSI in pinctrl must use `NRF_PSEL_DRIVE(HIGH)` (or equivalent `nordic,drive-mode`).
- **NCS v2.9.0 board name**: use the hierarchical form `nrf5340dk/nrf5340/cpuapp` (v2.7+ convention).
- **Sysbuild auto-merge**: `SB_CONFIG_NETCORE_IPC_RADIO=y` + `SB_CONFIG_NETCORE_IPC_RADIO_BT_HCI_IPC=y` automatically builds the net-core image and produces `merged.hex`.
