### Technical notes
- **Intel HEX file size cannot judge merge success**: sysbuild's merged.hex may be smaller than a single-core hex (record merging + address-layout optimisation). The correct check is the extended linear-address records.
- **sysbuild `rom_report`/`ram_report`**: must be invoked in the per-image build dir, not in the sysbuild root dir.
- **Static-struct zero init**: with a designated initializer, **unspecified fields are auto-zero-initialised** (C99 §6.7.8). Writing `.cs = {0}` is equivalent, but `-Werror=missing-braces` complains about nested structs — omitting is cleaner.
- **`-DCONFIG_*` passthrough to sysbuild**: `CONFIG_*` passed after `-- -D...` is dispatched by sysbuild to the app image's Kconfig and does not pollute the net core.
